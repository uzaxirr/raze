# Raze — Product Marketing Video

## Overview
Create a 30-second marketing video for Raze, a Telegram chatbot for Solana. The video should feel playful and character-driven — the purple ghost mascot IS Raze. The hook is: "Your crypto friend who never sleeps."

## Brand Essentials
- **Product:** Telegram chatbot for Solana — trade, research, track, and protect your crypto through natural conversation
- **Mascot:** Purple ghost (#9945FF) with big expressive eyes, wavy bottom edge, and pink cheek blush. Has 12 unique emotion variants with accessories (detective hat, sunglasses, telescope, etc.)
- **Colors:** Purple #9945FF (primary), Green #14F195, Pink #FF8FCE, Gold #FFD166, Red #CC0000, Off-white #FAFAFE background
- **Typography:** Space Grotesk bold for headlines, Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for code/crypto addresses
- **Tone:** Savage, sarcastic, crypto-native. Lowercase energy. Like a friend who roasts you but is actually helpful.
- **Visual style:** Light mode, soft pastel gradients, frosted glass cards, subtle grain texture, clean and editorial

## Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: Hook (0-3s)
**Visual:** Purple ghost mascot floats into frame center, waving with both arms. Sparkle particles animate around it. Soft purple gradient background (#EDE8FF → #E4DCFF).
**Text:** Large bold headline animates in: "Your crypto friend who never sleeps"
**Motion:** Ghost has idle floating animation (gentle up/down bob). Eyes blink once. Text slides up with ease-out.
**Audio cue:** Upbeat lo-fi electronic intro

### Scene 2: The Problem (3-7s)
**Visual:** Quick rapid cuts showing the pain of current crypto UX:
- Cut 1: Phantom wallet app icon → Jupiter swap interface → Birdeye charts → DexScreener → Telegram groups — all flashing in sequence (0.4s each)
- Cut 2: Ghost mascot appears looking exhausted/frustrated, arms down
**Text:** "Too many tabs. Too many apps." in bold Space Grotesk, center screen
**Motion:** Fast cuts with slight zoom on each app. Text punches in hard. Ghost shakes head.

### Scene 3: The Solution Reveal (7-10s)
**Visual:** Clean transition to a Telegram chat interface. The ghost mascot's face IS the bot profile picture (white circle, purple ghost inside). Chat header shows "Raze" with "online" status.
**Text in chat:** User types "what's in my wallet?" — Raze replies: "45.2 sol, some usdc and bonk. not terrible for once"
**Text overlay:** "Just type what you want." in muted text below
**Motion:** Telegram UI slides in from bottom. Messages appear with typing indicator, then pop in. Ghost mascot (dollar eyes variant) peeks from left side of phone.

### Scene 4: Feature Montage — Trading (10-14s)
**Visual:** Telegram chat continues. Background morphs to purple gradient.
**Chat exchange:** User: "swap 5 SOL to USDC" → Raze: "5 sol → 674.50 usdc via jupiter. send it?" with confirm/cancel buttons → "done. 674.50 usdc in your wallet"
**Ghost:** Winking variant with sunglasses appears, gives thumbs up
**Text overlay:** "Trade in one message" — slides in from right
**Motion:** Swap confirmation animates with a satisfying green checkmark. Background gradient shifts.

### Scene 5: Feature Montage — Intelligence (14-18s)
**Visual:** Chat shows token research. Background morphs to green gradient.
**Chat exchange:** User: "is BONK safe?" → Small purple text "checked security, holders, momentum, sentiment..." → Raze replies with token data card + "you're late tho, 25% off ATH"
**Ghost:** Detective variant (black fedora hat, magnifying glass) appears from top
**Text overlay:** "Thinks before it speaks" — frosted glass pill slides in
**Motion:** Data card builds up row by row. Magnifying glass ghost peers at the data.

### Scene 6: Feature Montage — Protection (18-22s)
**Visual:** Dark/dramatic moment. Background morphs to red/pink gradient. A notification banner slides in.
**Chat:** Raze sends unprompted at "3:47 AM": "yo wake up. XYZ Lending authority just changed. you have $4,200 in there. pull it NOW" with red withdraw button
**Ghost:** Shocked/alert variant with wide eyes and sweat drops
**Text overlay:** "Watches while you sleep" — appears with urgency
**Motion:** Phone vibrates subtly. Red alert color pulses. Timestamp "3:47 AM" is prominent.

### Scene 7: Memory & Personality (22-26s)
**Visual:** Quick cuts showing Raze's personality across different conversations:
- "oh btw your bonk is up 35% since you bought last tuesday" (ghost remembers)
- "your favorite whale is cooking again. want me to ape in like usual?" (ghost knows preferences)
- "filtered how you like it — no micro caps, no bundled trash" (ghost learned risk appetite)
**Ghost:** Different emotion variants flash with each cut — dollar eyes, spy mask, heart eyes
**Text overlay:** "Remembers everything. Learns your style." in center
**Motion:** Messages slide in rapidly with satisfying animations. Ghost emotions change with each cut.

### Scene 8: CTA Finale (26-30s)
**Visual:** Clean off-white background. Ghost mascot celebrating with both arms raised, sparkles and confetti particles around it. Stars twinkle.
**Text:** "Your on-chain intelligence, one message away" — large bold headline, center
**CTA:** Purple pill button "Start Chatting" with subtle glow animation
**Below CTA:** Small logos/text: "Solana · Telegram · Jupiter"
**Ghost:** Celebrating variant with arms up, does a small bounce animation (scale 0.9 → 1.05 → 1)
**Motion:** Everything converges to center. Ghost bounces in. Button pulses with glow. Fade to Raze logo (ghost face + "raze" wordmark).

## Technical Requirements
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **Frame rate:** 60fps for smooth animations
- **Color space:** sRGB
- **Background:** Light mode throughout (off-white #FAFAFE base with pastel gradient overlays)

## Key Visual References
- The ghost mascot SVGs are attached as image references
- The landing page at localhost:3002 shows the exact visual system
- Telegram chat mockups should match the style on the landing page (teal header #517DA2, light blue chat area #C8D9E6, green user bubbles #EEFFDE, white bot bubbles)

## What NOT to Do
- No generic AI/chatbot imagery
- No dark mode or dark backgrounds (except Scene 6 alert moment which uses pink/red)
- No corporate SaaS energy — this is a degen product with attitude
- No slow, boring reveals — keep the energy up
- No stock crypto charts or Bitcoin logos — this is Solana only
- No voiceover that sounds like a narrator — if any VO, it should sound like Raze (casual, sarcastic)
- No purple-on-dark gradient cliches
