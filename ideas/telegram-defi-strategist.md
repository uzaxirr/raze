# Telegram DeFi Strategist Bot

## Status: PARKED — good idea, not pursuing

## Concept
AI-powered Telegram bot that executes multi-step DeFi strategies on Solana via natural language. Users describe a strategy, multi-agent orchestrator (reuse pocketwallet engine) breaks it into sub-tasks and runs autonomously.

## Example
```
User: DCA 100 USDC/week into SOL, stake half on best-yield LST, compound weekly
Bot: Creates yield-hunter.sol with DCA Agent + Yield Agent + Compounder + Risk Monitor
```

## Differentiation
- Trojan/BONKbot = single swaps. This = multi-step autonomous strategies
- Kamino/Marinade vaults = hardcoded. This = custom natural language strategies
- Each strategy gets a .sol domain (SNS track) for onchain transparency

## Revenue
- 0.5–1% per operation (industry standard)
- 200 users × $500 avg × 4 ops/month = $3K/mo revenue at 0.75%

## Market Context
- Telegram bots: $23.4B volume in 2025, 74% of Solana trading
- BONKbot: $4.35M/month in fees
- Trojan: $24.2B lifetime volume, 2M users, 1% fee
- Solana Q1 2026: 80M SOL TVL ATH

## Why It Works

- Superteam distribution

## Why Parked
